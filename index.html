Does Aquaflame offer 24/7 emergency service?
Yes. Aquaflame operates 24 hours a day, 7 days a week including weekends and holidays. Call (778) 999-4422 any time for emergency furnace repair, heat pump service, or any HVAC issue across Surrey and the Fraser Valley.
How much is the BC heat pump rebate?
Qualifying BC homeowners can receive up to $6,000 from BC Hydro, up to $11,000 from FortisBC, and up to $4,000 from CleanBC — a combined total of up to $21,000. Aquaflame handles every step of the application at no extra charge.

Yes. All Aquaflame technicians hold Red Seal certification and are fully licensed and insured to perform HVAC work in British Columbia. We comply with all BC Technical Safety standards.
What areas does Aquaflame serve?
Aquaflame serves Surrey, Langley, Coquitlam, Richmond, Delta, White Rock, Maple Ridge, and the broader Fraser Valley. Call (778) 999-4422 for same-day service across all these areas.
How often should I service my furnace?
Annual furnace maintenance is recommended, ideally in early fall before heating season. Regular tune-ups extend system life, catch small problems before they become expensive, and keep your furnace running safely and efficiently.
